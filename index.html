Meh nak ajar cara buat MATCHAA MOCHIIIII

Bahan bahanyaaaaaa

  • 1 1/2 cups sweet rice flour

  • 1 500g of strawberry

  • 1 cup sugar

  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

  • 1 1/2 teaspoons matcha powder

  • 1 cup water

  • 3/4 cup coconut milk

  • 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • Cornstarch (for dusting)

    Cara caranyaaaaaaaaaaaa

  2. Step 2: In a medium bowl, whisk together the rice flour, sugar, baking powder, and matcha powder.

  3. Step 3: In a separate bowl, whisk together the water, coconut milk, and vanilla extract. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and whisk until smooth. In a separate bowl, whisk together the water, coconut milk, and vanilla extract. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and whisk until smooth.

  4. Step 4: Pour the batter into the pan, cover tightly with foil, and bake for 60 to 80 minutes, until the top is set.

  5. Step 5: Remove the foil, let it cool, and then re-cover with foil and let it sit at room temperature overnight.

  6. Step 6: Turn the mochi onto a cutting board and cut small cubes with a sharp knife. In this part, insert a strawberry. Coat all over with cornstarch.
